# Retail Fashion Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

The **Retail Fashion Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to evaluate brand performance, pricing strategies, discount trends, and profitability within the fashion retail industry. The dashboard transforms retail sales data into actionable insights that help businesses understand product performance and optimize pricing decisions.

The project demonstrates how Business Intelligence can support retail organizations by analyzing pricing behavior, profit margins, product variety, and brand competitiveness.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses must continuously balance competitive pricing with profitability while offering attractive discounts to customers.

This project was developed to answer key business questions such as:

- Which brands generate the highest profits?
- Which brands offer the largest discounts?
- Which brands have the widest product variety?
- How do pricing strategies affect profitability?
- Which brands achieve the highest average selling price?

## Key Insights

- Profit margins differ significantly across brands.
- Some brands rely heavily on discounts while maintaining competitive sales.
- Premium-priced brands generally generate higher average selling prices.
- Product variety varies considerably between brands, influencing market presence.
